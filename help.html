GAMEPLAY
How To Play

Move all four pawns from your home to the finish before your opponents do.

  • Roll to leave home, then advance along your track.
  • Block, chase, and time your moves to stay safe and finish fast.

On your turn, roll the dice and move one pawn by the rolled value.

  • If you have multiple valid moves, choose the pawn strategically.
  • Some modes grant extra turns on special rolls.

Landing on an opponent may capture them and send their pawn back home (mode dependent).

  • Safe tiles protect your pawn from capture.
  • Try to keep at least one pawn in a safe position as a fallback.
SOCIAL
Friends And Match-Up

Open the Friends page and send a friend request using a player name or tag.

  • Requests can only be sent to players who have an in-game account.
  • Incoming requests show up under Requests where you can Accept or Reject.

Use the Online filter to see friends who are currently available.

  • If a friend is online, you can start a match-up directly from their card.
  • If they are offline, you can still send messages or invite later (UI ready for expansion).

Type into the search bar to find players. If the player exists, you can send a request.

  • No account found means the request option stays disabled.
  • Use exact tags for best results (example: Name#1024).
STORE
Shop, Dice, And Pawns

Use the Shop tabs to browse dice and pawn collections, then purchase a design.

  • Owned items stay marked as owned.
  • Your selection can be applied in your loadout (UI ready for expansion).

Coins are earned through play and rewards, then spent on cosmetics.

  • Limited deals may appear as notifications.
  • Cosmetics do not affect gameplay outcomes.
SUPPORT
Troubleshooting

Friend requests are only available for players that exist in the game directory.

  • Double-check spelling and tag format.
  • If the player is not found, ask them to create an account first.

The notification pip appears when you have unread notifications.

  • Open Notifications and mark items as read.
  • If needed, clear all to reset the inbox.

This UI stores demo data locally in your browser.

  • Clearing site data will reset friends, requests, shop ownership, and notifications.
  • Use this if you want a fresh demo state.
